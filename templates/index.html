Emotions can be combined to form different feelings, much like colors can be mixed to create other shades.

  • Happiness

    Happiness is often defined as a pleasant emotional state that is characterized by feelings of contentment, joy, gratification, satisfaction, and well-being.

  • Fear

    Fear is a powerful emotion that can also play an important role in survival. Muscles become tense, heart rate, respiration increases, mind becomes more alert.

  • Sadness

    Sadness is often defined as a transient emotional state characterized by feelings of disappointment, grief, hopelessness, disinterest, and dampened mood.

  • Anger

    Anger can be a powerful emotion characterized by feelings of hostility, agitation, frustration, and antagonism towards others. Like fear, anger can play a part in body's fight or flight response.
